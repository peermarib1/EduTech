Karnataka MBBS Admission 2024-25: Overview

The Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) will be conducting Bihar MBBS Admission 2024-25 in May/June 2024-25. The application form for the same will be made available on the official website of BCECEB. Eligible candidates are required to fill and submit the form before the deadline. The selection of candidates for counselling will be done on the basis of their NEET scores. The counselling process will include document verification and seat allotment. The admission will be granted to the candidates after the allotment of seats in the participating institutes.

For Bihar MBBS admission 2024-25 in Govt colleges of Bihar only Bihar resident students or students having domicile of Bihar can apply. However, in private medical colleges of Bihar, every student who qualified NEET 2024-25 and is a resident of India can apply. In Bihar Private Medical colleges, the NRI quota is also available where NRI Category Students can apply.

In Bihar, there are a total of 21 Medical Colleges offering MBBS courses and accepting NEET UG. Among these, 13 are Government Medical Colleges (including 1 AIIMS and 0 JIPMER), while 8 are Private Medical Colleges.

There are a total of 2765 seats for Bihar MBBS. Among these, 1615 seats are reserved for Government Medical Colleges (including AIIMS and JIPMER), and 1150 seats are reserved for Private Medical Colleges.

MBBS Admission Bihar 2023: NEET CUT-OFF

There are two different sorts of NEET Cut Off: NEET Qualifying and NEET Admission Cut Off. The bare minimum score needed to Qualify the NEET Exam is known as the NEET Cut-off. The final rank or score for which the last admission will be awarded in respective medical college is the NEET Admission Cut-off.
